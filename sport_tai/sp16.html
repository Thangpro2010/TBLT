The last stretch of Maradona



A copy of the 1986 World Cup gold trophy was placed next to Maradona's coffin near the end of the visit at the presidential palace of Argentina - Casa Rosada, Buenos Aires on November 26.


In Argentina, the legend born in 1960 is considered a Saint, because of the way he inspired and contributed to the team to win the World Cup 1986. At the Mexican tournament at that time, Maradona scored five goals and ants. created another five, out of a total of 14 that Argentina scored on the journey to conquer the Golden Cup. Photo: Presidency Before that, from about 17:00, the police began to block the flow of people into Casa Rosada to end the visit. Chaos happened, because before that hundreds of thousands of people have flocked here to wait to bid farewell to Maradona.


The Maradona visit was originally scheduled to last for November 26 and 27. But chaos broke out continuously. The line of people was too crowded, impatient and clashed with the authorities, forcing the anti-police police to use batons, rubber bullets, tear gas and even tornado vehicles to suppress. The Maradona family therefore decided to end the visit early on the same evening. Photo: Clarin
