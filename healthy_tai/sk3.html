How to eat garlic properly?



According to Ayurveda (Indian traditional medicine), garlic's medicinal benefits make it a powerful spice that can naturally lower triglyceride levels in the body and improve heart health, making it easy. Diabetes control and immune booster. However, the strong aroma and taste of garlic is not pleasant when eaten raw. So here are some simple ways that you can get the maximum benefit of garlic and consume garlic properly, according to Times of India.


Garlic contains many good antioxidants that help reduce oxidative stress, help repair cell damage due to the presence of free radicals in the body. In addition, garlic is also an excellent source of minerals such as phosphorus, calcium, potassium, iron and copper. It is also a good source of vitamin C and vitamin B6 (pyridoxine).


The presence of vitamin C and iron in garlic naturally boosts the immune system and gives the body abundant strength and helps to build resistance against seasonal infections. Therefore, adding garlic to your daily diet can help keep your body warm during the cold season. So prepare yourself for the winter cold and provide your body with the necessary nutrients by adding garlic to your winter diet in some simple ways.
